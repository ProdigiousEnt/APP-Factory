Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

VibePaper ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Prompts and Text Input: We collect the creative prompts and text descriptions you enter to generate wallpapers.
  • Reference Images: If you choose to upload reference photos, we process these images to generate AI wallpapers.
  • Generated Content: We store metadata about the wallpapers you create (prompts, timestamps, configuration settings).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifiers: We collect device identifiers for app functionality and subscription management via RevenueCat.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including generation counts and feature usage.
  • Technical Data: We collect technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI Generation Services: Process your prompts and reference images using Google Gemini AI to generate custom wallpapers.
  • Manage Subscriptions: Track subscription status and entitlements via RevenueCat.
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience.
  • Provide Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues.
  • Enforce Usage Limits: Track generation counts for free tier users.

3. Third-Party Services

We share information with the following third-party service providers:

3.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Data Shared: Your text prompts and reference images (if provided) are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate wallpapers.
  • Purpose: AI-powered wallpaper generation.
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 RevenueCat

  • Data Shared: Device identifiers, subscription status, and purchase information.
  • Purpose: Subscription management and in-app purchase processing.
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

3.3 Supabase (Optional - if used for cloud sync)

  • Data Shared: Wallpaper metadata (prompts, timestamps, configuration settings).
  • Purpose: Cloud synchronization of your wallpaper library.
  • Privacy Policy: Supabase Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Generated wallpapers and metadata are primarily stored locally on your device using the Capacitor Filesystem API.
  • Cloud Metadata: If cloud sync is enabled, wallpaper metadata (not the images themselves) may be stored in Supabase.
  • Retention Period: We retain your data for as long as you use the app. You can delete your wallpapers and data at any time using the "Clear All" function in the app.
  • Account Deletion: If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device. Cloud metadata can be deleted by contacting support.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view all wallpapers and metadata stored in the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual wallpapers or clear all data using the in-app "Clear All" button.
  • Opt-Out: You can choose not to provide reference images or use cloud sync features.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

6. Children's Privacy

VibePaper is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure API authentication for third-party services
  • Local device storage using iOS secure filesystem APIs

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country. By using VibePaper, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
