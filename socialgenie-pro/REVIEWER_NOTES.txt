SocialGenie Pro - AI Social Media Content Generator

IMPORTANT: Usage limits are temporarily DISABLED for testing. They will be re-enabled immediately after approval.

HOW TO TEST:

1. GENERATE CONTENT
   - Enter any idea in the text field (e.g., "A coffee shop opening")
   - Select a tone (Professional, Witty, Urgent, or Inspirational)
   - Choose platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram)
   - Select "1K Fidelity" (free tier)
   - Tap "Generate Multi-Channel"
   - Wait 10-30 seconds for AI generation

2. TEST SUBSCRIPTION FLOW
   - Tap "PRO" badge in header, OR
   - Tap "Upgrade" button in the banner, OR
   - Select "2K Fidelity" (triggers paywall)
   - Paywall displays subscription details
   - Note: Sandbox purchases won't complete until approved

3. TEST SHARE FEATURE
   - Tap share button on any platform card
   - iOS share sheet appears with image file
   - Can save to Photos or share to other apps

4. TEST ARCHIVES
   - Tap "ARCHIVES" in header
   - View previously generated content
   - All stored locally on device

MONETIZATION:
- Free: 3 generations/week, 1K images
- Pro: Unlimited generations, 2K images ($39.99/year)

PRIVACY:
- No data collection
- All content stored locally on device
- No analytics or tracking

Usage limits will be re-enabled by uncommenting lines 162-174 in App.tsx.

TECHNICAL:
- AI: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash (text) and Gemini 2.5 Flash Image (images)
- Known limitation: Images generate as square (1:1) due to AI model constraints
