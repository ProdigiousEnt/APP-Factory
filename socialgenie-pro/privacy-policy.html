Privacy Policy for SocialGenie Pro

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Introduction

SocialGenie Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our service:

  • Device ID: An anonymous identifier used solely for subscription management through RevenueCat.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as generation count, to enforce free tier limits and improve app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Managing your subscription status
  • Enforcing weekly generation limits for free tier users
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Providing customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for data storage and RevenueCat for subscription management. All data is anonymous and not linked to your personal identity.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Supabase: For usage tracking
  • Google Gemini AI: For content generation (your prompts are processed but not stored)

Your Generated Content

All content you generate using SocialGenie Pro belongs to you. We do not store, share, or use your generated posts or images for any purpose other than displaying them to you within the app.

Data Retention

We retain usage data for 90 days. Subscription data is managed by RevenueCat according to their retention policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Access the data we have collected
  • Opt out of data collection (note: this may limit app functionality)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
