Introduction

Welcome to CityScope AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use the CityScope AI mobile application (the "App").

By using CityScope AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

Photos and Images

  • When you upload photos to create AI postcards, we process these images using Google's Gemini AI service
  • Photos are temporarily processed and are not permanently stored on our servers
  • You control which photos you upload to the App

User-Generated Content

  • Text prompts and descriptions you provide for postcard customization
  • This content is processed by our AI service to generate your personalized postcards

2. Automatically Collected Information

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version

Usage Data

  • Features you use within the App
  • Number of postcards generated
  • App interactions and navigation patterns
  • Crash reports and performance data

Subscription Information

  • Purchase history for in-app subscriptions
  • Subscription status (free or Pro)
  • Transaction identifiers (managed by RevenueCat)

3. Location Information

We do NOT collect precise location data. Any location information displayed in the App (e.g., landmark locations) is derived from:

  • Photo metadata (EXIF data) that you choose to share
  • AI analysis of landmark images you upload
  • User-provided text descriptions

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • AI Processing: To identify landmarks and generate artistic postcards using Google Gemini AI
  • History & Gallery: To save your created postcards locally on your device
  • Audio Narration: To generate and play AI-powered audio tours of landmarks

Subscription Management

  • Payment Processing: To manage your Pro subscription via RevenueCat and Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Access Control: To determine which features you can access (free vs. Pro)
  • Purchase Restoration: To restore your subscription across devices

App Improvement

  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve features
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Feature Development: To prioritize new features based on usage patterns

Data Retention

Photos and Images

  • Temporary Processing: Photos uploaded for AI processing are temporarily cached and deleted after processing is complete
  • Local Storage: Generated postcards are saved locally on your device and can be deleted by you at any time
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not permanently store your original photos on our servers

User Data

  • Active Users: Your postcard history and preferences are retained while you actively use the App
  • Account Deletion: If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days
  • Subscription Data: Purchase history is retained as required by law for tax and accounting purposes (typically 7 years)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest
  • Access Controls: We limit access to your personal information to authorized personnel only
  • Third-Party Security: We carefully vet all third-party services for security and privacy compliance

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Portability

  • View Your Data: You can view your postcard history within the App
  • Export Data: Contact us to request a copy of your data in a portable format

Deletion

  • Delete Postcards: You can delete individual postcards from your history within the App
  • Delete Account: Contact us to request full account deletion
  • Right to Be Forgotten: We will delete your personal data upon request (subject to legal retention requirements)

Subscription Management

  • Cancel Anytime: Manage your subscription through the App Store settings
  • Refunds: Request refunds through Apple's App Store support

Children's Privacy

CityScope AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. By using CityScope AI, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and displaying a notice within the App.
