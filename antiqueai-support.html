About AntiqueAI

AntiqueAI is your expert-in-pocket for antique and artifact identification. Simply snap a photo of any antique, collectible, or vintage item, and our AI provides instant expert-level analysis including identification, authenticity insights, historical context, and valuation guidance. Perfect for collectors, estate sales, flea markets, and anyone curious about the history and value of vintage items.

  • Expert tips for research and appraisal

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI identification work?
Simply take a photo of your antique or artifact, and our AI (powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash) analyzes the image to identify the item type, historical period, materials, authenticity markers, and estimated value. The AI provides expert-level analysis in seconds.
How accurate is the identification?
AntiqueAI uses advanced AI trained on vast amounts of historical and cultural data. While highly accurate, the app is an educational tool and should not replace professional appraisal services for high-value items or legal authentication.
What types of items can I identify?
AntiqueAI can identify a wide range of items including furniture, pottery, glassware, jewelry, artwork, coins, stamps, vintage toys, collectibles, and more. The AI works best with clear, well-lit photos showing details and any markings.
What's included in the free version?
The free version includes 3 lifetime scans. This allows you to try the full expert analysis feature before upgrading to Pro.
What's included in Pro?
Pro includes 100 identifications per month, unlimited scan history, and the ability to save favorites for quick reference. It's $4.99/month.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscriptions in your iPhone settings, find AntiqueAI, and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Is my data private?
Yes! Antique images are processed securely by Google Gemini AI and are not stored permanently on our servers. Your scan history is stored locally on your device only.
Can I get a professional appraisal?
AntiqueAI provides educational guidance and preliminary identification. For legal authentication, insurance purposes, or high-value items, we recommend consulting a certified professional appraiser in your area.
