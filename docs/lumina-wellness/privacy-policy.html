Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Introduction

Lumina Wellness ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Health and Fitness Data

We collect the following health-related information that you provide:

  • Menstrual cycle dates (period start and end dates)
  • Symptom logs (mood, energy levels, physical symptoms)
  • Personal notes and observations
  • Wellness preferences and goals

Device Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (for data persistence across sessions)
  • App usage data (features accessed, interaction patterns)
  • Technical information (app version, device type)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Track your menstrual cycle, predict future periods, and log symptoms
  • Personalize Your Experience: Generate AI-powered wellness insights tailored to your current hormone phase
  • Improve Our Service: Analyze app usage to enhance features and user experience
  • Maintain Data Persistence: Store your data securely so you can access it across app sessions

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our app:

Google Gemini API

We use Google's Gemini AI to generate personalized wellness insights based on your cycle phase and symptoms. Your health data is sent to Google's servers for processing but is not stored by Google or used for any purpose other than generating your insights.

Supabase

We use Supabase to securely store your health data. Your data is encrypted and stored on Supabase's servers. We implement Row-Level Security to ensure your data is isolated and accessible only to you.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your purchase information but does not have access to your health data.

Data Storage and Security

Your health data is:

  • Stored securely in Supabase with encryption
  • Isolated using Row-Level Security policies
  • Accessible only via your unique device identifier
  • Never sold or shared with third parties for advertising

Data Retention

We retain your health data for as long as you use the app. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at appfactory1970@gmail.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the app at any time

Children's Privacy

Lumina Wellness is not intended for children under 12. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 12, please contact us immediately.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal or health data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your data with data brokers
  • Track you across other apps or websites

International Users

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. By using Lumina Wellness, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
