About Lumina Wellness

Lumina Wellness is your personal AI health companion for cycle tracking, wellness insights, and hormone health. Our app helps you understand your body better by tracking your menstrual cycle and providing personalized recommendations based on your current hormone phase.

✨ Features

  • Smart Cycle Tracking: Log your period and get accurate predictions for your next cycle
  • AI Wellness Insights: Receive personalized advice tailored to your current hormone phase
  • Daily Symptom Logging: Track mood, energy, and physical symptoms
  • Personalized Recommendations: Get nutrition, exercise, and self-care tips
  • Pelvic Floor Exercises: Access guided exercises for pelvic health
  • Beautiful Interface: Enjoy a calming, intuitive design

Getting Started

1. Set Up Your Profile

When you first open Lumina Wellness, you'll be asked to enter:

  • Your last period start date
  • Average cycle length (default: 28 days)
  • Average period length (default: 5 days)
  • Your tracking mode (Cycle, Fertility, or Menopause)

2. Log Your Symptoms

Tap the "Log Symptoms" button to record:

  • Mood (Happy, Neutral, Sad, Anxious, Irritable)
  • Energy level (High, Medium, Low)
  • Physical symptoms (Cramps, Headache, Bloating, etc.)
  • Personal notes

3. Get AI Insights

Tap "Get AI Insights" to receive personalized wellness advice based on your current cycle phase. The AI considers your symptoms and hormone phase to provide tailored recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes! Your health data is stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How accurate are the predictions?

Lumina Wellness uses your historical data to predict your next period. Accuracy improves as you log more cycles. Remember that cycle length can vary naturally.

What's included in Lumina Pro?

Lumina Pro ($4.99/month) unlocks:

  • Unlimited symptom logging
  • Unlimited AI wellness insights
  • Advanced period predictions
  • Personalized health recommendations

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your Lumina Pro subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Lumina Wellness"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Troubleshooting

App won't load my data

Try these steps:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Close and reopen the app
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the issue persists, contact support

AI insights aren't working

Ensure you have:

  • An active internet connection
  • Logged at least one symptom
  • Set up your cycle information

Subscription issues

If you're having trouble with your subscription:

  1. Verify your payment method in Settings → Apple ID → Payment & Shipping
  2. Try restoring purchases in the app
  3. Contact Apple Support for billing issues
