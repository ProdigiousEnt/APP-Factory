Privacy Policy for ResumeTailor Pro

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

1. Introduction

ResumeTailor Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Resume Content: Text from your resume that you input for analysis
  • Job Descriptions: Job posting text you provide for matching analysis

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous device ID for analytics
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Subscription Status: Information about your Pro subscription (managed by RevenueCat)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • AI Analysis: Processing your resume and job descriptions through Google Gemini AI to provide tailored recommendations
  • App Functionality: Tracking usage limits (3 free analyses, 1,000 Pro analyses per month)
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with the app to improve features
  • Subscription Management: Managing your Pro subscription and billing

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Usage tracking data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: Your resume content and job descriptions are NOT stored on our servers
  • Temporary Processing: Resume text is sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis and is not retained after processing
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: AI-powered resume analysis and job matching
  • Data Shared: Resume text and job descriptions (not linked to your identity)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.2 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription management and billing
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

  • Resume Content: Not retained (processed in real-time only)
  • Usage Data: Stored locally on your device until you delete the app
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for up to 2 years

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics by not using the app
  • Data Portability: Export your resume analysis results (copy to clipboard feature)

8. Children's Privacy

ResumeTailor Pro is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
