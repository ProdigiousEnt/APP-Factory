About ResumeTailor Pro

ResumeTailor Pro is an AI-powered resume optimization tool that helps job seekers tailor their resumes to specific job postings. Using advanced AI technology (Google Gemini 2.5 Flash), the app analyzes your resume against job descriptions and provides:

  • Job alignment scores (0-100%)
  • Strengths and weaknesses analysis
  • Actionable improvement suggestions
  • Tailored resume content optimized for the job

How to Use ResumeTailor Pro

Step 1: Enter Job Description

Paste the full text of the job posting you're applying for. Include requirements, responsibilities, and qualifications.

Step 2: Enter Your Resume

Paste your current resume text or upload a .txt file. Include your experience, education, and skills.

Step 3: Analyze & Tailor

Tap "Analyze & Tailor" to get your results. The AI will process your information and provide:

  • Critique Tab: Matching score, strengths, gaps, and suggestions
  • Tailored Resume Tab: Optimized headline, summary, and bullet points

Step 4: Copy & Use

Use the "Copy Content" button to copy the tailored resume to your clipboard, then paste it into your resume builder.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my resume data stored or shared?

A: No. Your resume content is processed in real-time by Google Gemini AI and is NOT stored on our servers. After analysis, the data is discarded. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: How accurate is the AI analysis?

A: ResumeTailor Pro uses Google Gemini 2.5 Flash, a production-stable AI model. While highly accurate, we recommend using the suggestions as guidance and applying your professional judgment.

Q: Can I use this for multiple job applications?

A: Yes! That's exactly what it's designed for. Analyze your resume against each job posting to create tailored versions for every application.

Q: What file formats are supported?

A: Currently, you can paste text directly or upload .txt files. We recommend copying text from your Word/PDF resume and pasting it into the app.

Q: I've used my 3 free analyses. What now?

A: Upgrade to Pro ($4.99/month) for 1,000 analyses per month. Tap the upgrade prompt when you reach your limit.

Q: How do I restore my Pro subscription?

A: If you reinstall the app or switch devices, tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall screen to restore your Pro access.

Q: The analysis failed. What should I do?

A: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, try shortening your resume/job description or contact support.

Troubleshooting

App Won't Load

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force quit and restart the app
  • Restart your iPhone
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store

Purchase Issues

  • Verify your payment method in App Store settings
  • Try "Restore Purchases" on the paywall
  • Contact Apple Support for billing issues

Analysis Takes Too Long

  • Ensure strong Wi-Fi or cellular connection
  • Try shortening your input text
  • Wait a few moments and try again
