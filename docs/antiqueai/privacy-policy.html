Privacy Policy for AntiqueAI

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Introduction

AntiqueAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Device ID: Anonymous identifier for subscription management via RevenueCat
  • Usage Data: Scan count to enforce free tier limits (3 lifetime scans)
  • Antique Images: Processed by AI but not stored permanently on our servers

How We Use Your Information

  • Managing subscription status
  • Enforcing usage limits (3 free scans, 100/month Pro)
  • Processing antique images with AI for identification
  • Improving app performance and accuracy

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Google Gemini AI: Antique image analysis and identification

Data Retention

Usage data: Stored locally on your device. Antique images: Processed in real-time by Google Gemini AI and not stored permanently on our servers. Your scan history is stored locally on your device only.

Your Rights

You can request deletion or access to your data by contacting us. You can also delete your local scan history directly from the app.

Children's Privacy

AntiqueAI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
